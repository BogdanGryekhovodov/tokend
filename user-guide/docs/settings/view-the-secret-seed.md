

# View the secret seed

Commonly, you might need a secret seed for two things: log in to the offline application and [recover your account](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-SVmZLVLLid_Ky98yPI1n90IiBOxTYogzQRfAijph0k/edit?usp=drive_web&ouid=104378834969986631558). To see your secret seed, you:



1.  In the navigation bar, go to **Settings**
1.  Go to **Secret seed **tab
1.  There you can see your secret seed _(**Important: **never expose your secret seed to anyone. A person who has the seed controls the account)_
